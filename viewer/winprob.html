About this estimate

Win probability is estimated from the rating difference using a logistic model calibrated so that a 600-point advantage (the USAU blowout threshold) corresponds to roughly 95% win probability. This is a statistical approximation — individual games depend on many factors not captured by ratings.

P(A wins) = 1 / (1 + exp(−(R_A − R_B) / 204))
